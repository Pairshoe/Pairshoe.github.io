Publications
DynaKV: Enabling Accurate and Efficient Long-Sequence LLM Decoding on Smartphones

Tuowei Wang*, Minxing Huang*, Fengzu Li, Ligeng Chen, Jinrui Zhang, Yaoxue Zhang, Ju Ren
[Paper] arXiv, 2025

We propose DynaKV, the first adaptive KVCache management approach designed to jointly address accuracy and efficiency for long-sequence LLM decoding on smartphones.

Jenga: Enhancing LLM Long-Context Fine-tuning with Contextual Token Sparsity

Tuowei Wang, Xingyu Chen, Kun Li, Ting Cao, Ju Ren, Yaoxue Zhang
[Paper] ATC, 2025

We propose Jenga, the first LLM fine-tuning system that explores and exploits a new token-level sparsity mechanism inherent in long-context scenarios, termed Contextual Token Sparsity.

Neuralink: Fast LLM Inference on Smartphones with Neuron Co-Activation Linking

Tuowei Wang*, Ruwen Fan*, Minxing Huang, Zixu Hao, Kun Li, Ting Cao, Youyou Lu, Yaoxue Zhang, Ju Ren
[Paper] ASPLOS, 2025

We propose Neuralink, a novel approach that accelerates LLM inference on smartphones by optimizing neuron placement in flash memory.

Long Exposure: Accelerating Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning for LLMs under Shadowy Sparsity

Tuowei Wang*, Kun Li*, Zixu Hao, Donglin Bai, Ju Ren, Yaoxue Zhang, Ting Cao, Mao Yang
[Paper] SC, 2024

We propose Long Exposure, an efficient system to accelerate parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) for LLMs under a nuanced form of sparsity we term Shadowy Sparsity.

Matryoshka: Optimization of Dynamic Diverse Quantum Chemistry Systems via Elastic Parallelism Transformation

Tuowei Wang, Kun Li, Donglin Bai, Fusong Ju, Leo Xia, Ting Cao, Ju Ren, Yaoxue Zhang, Mao Yang
[Paper] arXiv, 2024

We propose Matryoshka, a novel elastically-parallel technique for the efficient execution of quantum chemistry system with dynamic diversity on GPU.
